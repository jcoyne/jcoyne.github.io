What did we do to get here?

In the big picture, not much. we know what we want the interface to look like (ActiveFedora 7 + the features of Fedora 4) But there was a lot of small changes

Cheers! Sláinte! Prost!

We basically started at scratch at Hydra Connect 1 in January. At that point Cbeer had created an early version of the LDP gem. We had a bit of a come-to-jesus meeting where we raised the fact that lots of money had gone into Fedora 4, and fedora 3 was getting ready for End of Life, but we didn't have a plan for Hydra.

Bmaddy built the fedora_lens gem on top of that, a basic active-model ORM.

I worked to get ActiveRecord working with this fedora_lens gem, but it had a different api than datastreams, so we knew that we needed to ditch FedoraLens and just use ActiveTriples for both datastreams and object properties. we've largely got that done now.

Some of the more nucanced issues, versioning, fixity checking, I left by the wayside and Awead and Hector picked up, and those are largely done.

What's blocking us. Basically just finding the time to work on it.
